Data Center Concrete Solutions

Data center projects move fast and require contractors who understand the timeline, precision, and reliability demands. We specialize in concrete work that keeps your facility on schedule and operating smoothly.

Equipment Pad Removal & Demolition

When equipment moves, the old concrete footings and pads have to come out. We handle safe, efficient demolition and removal of existing concrete slabs, pads, and foundations. We work cleanly and can coordinate around your operational schedule.

New Equipment Foundation Pours

Once old concrete is cleared, we prepare the site and pour new precision foundations for relocated or new equipment. We build to exact specifications with proper loading capacity, drainage, and finish standards that equipment installers require.

Site Prep & Grading

Data center sites often require careful grading, drainage, and base preparation before concrete work begins. We handle site assessment, excavation, base material installation, and drainage to ensure your concrete foundation is built on solid ground.

High-Tolerance Flatness Work

Equipment installation demands level, flat concrete surfaces. We pour and finish to tight tolerance specifications so equipment sits correctly and operates reliably. Our experience with precision finishes means your foundation is ready for installation right away.
