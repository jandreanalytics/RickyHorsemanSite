About

About Ricky Horseman

Ricky Horseman provides concrete construction and site services for residential and commercial properties throughout the region. Based in Leesburg, Virginia, the company has built a long-standing reputation for dependable concrete work, specialty surfaces such as sport courts, and practical site support delivered with clear communication and consistent execution.

The scope of work includes foundations, flatwork, slabs, pads, and specialty concrete applications, along with broader site and property services. In addition to construction, the company provides professional snow removal and ice management services to support seasonal access and operations across private and commercial properties.

Ricky's approach to planning, coordination, and site conditions aligns closely with the requirements of large-scale and controlled-access environments, including data center campuses and facility-oriented projects. As the company continues to grow, services are being positioned to support these environments through disciplined processes, coordination with project teams, and an emphasis on reliability and access.

Whether supporting a residential property, a commercial site, or a complex facility environment, Ricky Horseman's work is grounded in experience, adaptability, and an understanding of how concrete and site services fit into larger project goals. That reputation even led to an on-screen feature in HGTV's "Curb Appeal: Charleston or Bust," where Ricky's craftsmanship was showcased for a national audience.
